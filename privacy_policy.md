II. DATA PRIVACY & COMPLIANCE

This application is designed to be fully compliant with data privacy regulations (like HIPAA and GDPR) by prohibiting the collection of personal medical data.

1. Prohibition of Protected Health Information (PHI)

Users are strictly prohibited from entering identifiable patient information into the application (including search fields, input forms, or AI queries). Prohibited data includes:

Real patient names, initials, or geographic subdivisions.

Dates of birth, admission, discharge, or death.

Medical record numbers (MRN) or health plan beneficiary numbers.

Biometric identifiers or photographic images.

2. Information Sharing & Third-Party AI Services

Queries: Queries entered into the AI Differential Generator feature are transmitted to external Large Language Model (LLM) providers (e.g., Google's Gemini API) for processing. We ensure this data is sent de-identified and is not linked back to individual users.

User Authentication: Basic, non-PHI data (user ID, session token, authentication status) is managed locally and used solely to grant access to the application.

Analytics: We collect aggregated, anonymous usage data (e.g., frequency of calculator use, page views) to maintain and improve the service.
